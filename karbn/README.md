## Karbn

This was early conceptual design for a comprehensive carbon footprint calculation platform.

### Design Goals

- Surface a large amount of complex data in an accessible and digestible way, from headline values to more tightly scoped data
- Clearly communicate confidence in data quality and accuracy
- Use progressive disclosure to keep users focussed on a specific data space without creating overwhelm
- Allow users to drill down into their data at the most granular level
- Create distinct intent areas (analysis, next actions)
- Create a sense of 'journey' as users added data to the platform to build a comprehensive insight into their carbon output
- Provide clear direction on what users were expected to do next, and provide discreet and simple to navigate step journeys
- Allow users to set and track goals as part of their carbon reduction activities.
